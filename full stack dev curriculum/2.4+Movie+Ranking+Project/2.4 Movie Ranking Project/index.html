The Best Movies According to Stanley The Hybrid Of Lagos

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. The Social Network

Released in 2010, The Social Network is a biographical drama film directed by David Fincher. It tells the story of the founding of Facebook and the legal battles that followed. The film stars Jesse Eisenberg as Mark Zuckerberg, the co-founder of Facebook, and features a compelling narrative that explores themes of ambition, friendship, and betrayal.

2. Wolf Of Wall Street

Released in 2013, Wolf of Wall Street is a biographical black comedy film directed by Martin Scorsese. It is based on the memoir of the same name by Jordan Belfort, a former stockbroker who was involved in a massive fraud scheme. The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Belfort and features an ensemble cast that includes Jonah Hill, Margot Robbie, and Matthew McConaughey.

3. Jobs

Released in 2013, Jobs is a biographical drama film directed by Danny Boyle. It tells the story of Steve Jobs, the co-founder and CEO of Apple Inc., and his journey from the early days of Apple to his return to the company in 1997. The film stars Ashton Kutcher as Steve Jobs and features a compelling narrative that explores themes of innovation, ambition, and personal relationships.
